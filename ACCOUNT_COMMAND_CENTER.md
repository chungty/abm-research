## Technical Implementation

### Account-Specific Data Synthesis
The system processes:
1. **Firmographics**: Company size, funding, industry position
2. **Trigger Events**: Recent announcements, job postings, leadership changes
3. **Contact Intelligence**: LinkedIn activity, role responsibilities, pain points
4. **Partnership Context**: Vendor relationships, warm intro opportunities
5. **Competitive Landscape**: Key differentiators and positioning

### Action Generation Engine
For each account, the system automatically generates:
- **Opportunity briefing** with strategic context
- **Priority contact ranking** with timing optimization
- **Message templates** personalized to recent triggers
- **Partnership angle recommendations** with specific intro paths
- **Competitive positioning** tailored to account situation

### Real-Time Updates
- **Trigger monitoring**: Continuous scanning for new opportunities
- **Contact activity tracking**: LinkedIn posts, job changes, content engagement
- **Competitive intelligence**: Market movements affecting target accounts
